# Karo

The largest city on [[locations/mesulea/miezo|Miezo]] and the seat of government for the [[factions/mesulea|Mesulean Lunar Empire]].

## Location

Situated on Miezo's inward hemisphere, within the limestone cave networks that characterize the Mesulea-facing side of the moon.

## Government

Karo houses the **capital buildings** of the MLE, including:
- The Regent's offices
- The Senate chambers
- Administrative ministries

## Population

Part of the 17% of Miezo's population that lives above ground, though much of the city's infrastructure extends into the underground cave networks that have been expanded over centuries.
