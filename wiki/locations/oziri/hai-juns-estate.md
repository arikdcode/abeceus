# Hai Jun's Estate

The residence of [[characters/hai-jun|Hai Jun]], Pirate Lord of the [[organizations/pirate-clans/spike-reavers|Spike Reavers]], located in Middle [[locations/oziri/zirta|Zirta]].

## Layout

The estate rests inside a dome. The ceiling is a massive screen displaying the night sky or [[locations/abeceus|Abeceus]] as seen from [[locations/atruna/index|Atruna]], depending on the time of day.
