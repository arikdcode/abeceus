# Docking Cells

The honeycomb-structured ship berths at [[locations/oziri/zirta/index|Zirta]].

## Structure

Like all [[locations/oziri/index|Oziri]] docks:
- Each cell houses a single ship
- Retractable doors on each cell (usually open during peacetime)
- Different sized cells for different craft

## Operations

- **Minor repairs**: Conducted by vacc-suited crews
- **Major repairs**: Cells can be pressurized
