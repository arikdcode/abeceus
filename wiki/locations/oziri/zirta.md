# Zirta

The largest city on Oziri, jointly managed by the entire [[factions/oziri-council|Oziri Council]].

## Layout

The city exists in a colossal chamber beneath the docks:
- **Floor area**: 20 Ã— 20 km
- **Height**: 2 km
- **Upper city**: Hangs from the ceiling
- **Lower city**: Rests on the floor

## The Zirta Docks

The largest dock on Oziri sits directly above the city, beneath the planetary shell. It attracts the most commercial traffic of any dock on Oziri.

Docks are structured in a honeycomb pattern:
- Each cell houses a single ship
- Retractable doors (usually open during peacetime)
- Cells can be pressurized for major repairs
