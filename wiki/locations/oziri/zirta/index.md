# Zirta

The largest city on [[locations/oziri/index|Oziri]], jointly managed by the entire [[factions/oziri-council|Oziri Council]].

## The Zirta Dock

The largest dock on Oziri sits directly above the city, beneath the planetary shell. It attracts the most commercial traffic of any dock on Oziri.

- [[locations/oziri/zirta/dock-district|Dock District]]
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/docking-cells|Docking Cells]]

## City Layout

The city exists in a **colossal chamber** beneath the docks:

| Dimension | Measurement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Floor area | 20 × 20 km |
| Height | 2 km |

## Upper Zirta

Hangs from the ceiling.

- [[locations/oziri/zirta/market-district|Market District]] — Vendors selling every conceivable good
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/commercial-district|Commercial District]]
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/hab-district|Hab District]] — Upper and middle class housing
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/warehouse-district|Warehouse District]]

## Middle Zirta

The transition zone between upper and lower city.

- [[locations/oziri/hai-juns-estate|Hai Jun's Estate]]

## Lower Zirta

Rests on the floor.

- [[locations/oziri/zirta/industrial-district|Industrial District]] — Manufacturing
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/urban-district|Urban District]] — Working class housing
- [[locations/oziri/zirta/slum-district|Slum District]] — The bottom of Oziri society
