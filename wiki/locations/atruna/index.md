## Background

The modern inhabitants of the wider Abecean system are descended from the original colonists of Atruna. After **The Scream**, Atruna retained scientific understanding in their databanks, but had to rebuild engineering and industrial expertise from scratch over centuries.

## Political Status

- Lost position as primary power centuries ago
- Maintains close diplomatic ties with former colonies (Mesulea, Nuilea)
- Considered **neutral ground** for ceremonies and diplomacy
- Mesulean and Nuilean governments integrate Atrunean ceremonial functions
