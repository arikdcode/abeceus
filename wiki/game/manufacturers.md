# Manufacturers & Corporations

The system's economy is driven by corporations and organizations that produce everything from warships to ration packs. Each manufacturer has its own identity, aligned interests, and market niche.

---

## Understanding Manufacturer Profiles

### Alignment

Manufacturers range from wholly-owned subsidiaries of governments to genuinely neutral multinationals:

| Alignment | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **State-Owned** | Government entity; products restricted to that power |
| **Closely Aligned** | Private but dependent on one power's contracts/protection |
| **Loosely Aligned** | Prefers one power but sells to others |
| **Multinational** | Operates across borders; cultivates neutrality |
| **Independent** | Small/regional; no significant political ties |
| **Black Market** | Operates outside legal channels |

### Size Categories

| Size | Annual Revenue | Employees | Notes |
|------|----------------|-----------|-------|
| **Megacorp** | 100B+ credits | 500,000+ | System-wide influence, diversified |
| **Major** | 10-100B | 50,000-500,000 | Dominant in sector |
| **Mid-Size** | 1-10B | 5,000-50,000 | Regional or niche leader |
| **Small** | 100M-1B | 500-5,000 | Specialized, local |
| **Boutique** | <100M | <500 | Artisan, custom work |

## Defense & Military

### Kinetic Weapons

#### Hammerstar Industries

**Size:** Major | **Alignment:** Closely Aligned (Nuilea) | **HQ:** Nuilea

The backbone of Nuilean military power. Hammerstar builds weapons for wars of attrition—designs that prioritize stopping power and durability over elegance. Their battle rifles have armed three generations of Nuilean marines.

**Character:** Industrial, brutal, reliable. The sound of a Hammerstar rifle is the sound of war.

**Signature Traits:**
- Heavy recoil, heavy damage
- Overbuilt construction—survives abuse
- Distinctive loud report
- Limited customization options
- Affordable for military contracts

**Products:**
- Battle rifles, DMRs
- Anti-materiel rifles
- Squad support weapons
- Shipboard point-defense guns
- Ammunition (Hammerstar-standard casings)

---

#### Kovac Armaments

**Size:** Mid-Size | **Alignment:** Loosely Aligned (Nuilea) | **HQ:** Nuilea

Where Hammerstar builds for the military, Kovac builds for everyone else. Their designs are simpler, cheaper, and more accessible—the weapons you find in every freehold homestead and merc armory. Not fancy, but they work.

**Character:** Blue-collar, practical, everyman. Kovac doesn't pretend to be elite.

**Signature Traits:**
- Budget-friendly pricing
- Simple maintenance
- No proprietary parts
- "Good enough" accuracy
- Huge aftermarket support

**Products:**
- Civilian rifles and shotguns
- Compact sidearms
- Budget SMGs
- Hunting weapons
- Security-grade equipment

---

#### Red Phoenix Manufacturing

**Size:** Mid-Size | **Alignment:** Multinational | **HQ:** Tian Cylinder (Freeholds)

Originally a Nuilean arms company, Red Phoenix relocated to the Freeholds after disagreements with military procurement. Now they sell to anyone with credits, specializing in weapons that walk the line between civilian and military capability.

**Character:** Pragmatic, mercenary, no questions asked. Your money is your passport.

**Signature Traits:**
- "Civilian" weapons that happen to be combat-ready
- Easy export paperwork
- Deniable procurement
- Solid quality, fair prices
- Discreet customer service

**Products:**
- "Sporting" rifles (suspiciously tactical)
- High-capacity sidearms
- Parts kits and receivers
- Ammunition in bulk

---

#### Thornfield Arms

**Size:** Small | **Alignment:** Closely Aligned (Atruna) | **HQ:** Atruna

Premium kinetic weapons for those who find Hammerstar too crude but energy weapons too unreliable. Thornfield builds beautiful, precise firearms for wealthy collectors, competitive shooters, and corporate security.

**Character:** Aristocratic, precise, traditional. The gentleman's gun maker.

**Signature Traits:**

### Energy Weapons

#### Angelico Arms

**Size:** Major | **Alignment:** Closely Aligned (Atruna) | **HQ:** Atruna

The pinnacle of directed-energy weapon design. Angelico builds weapons for those who can afford the best—armor-penetrating beams in sleek white-and-chrome housings. Temperamental, proprietary, and devastatingly effective.

**Character:** Elegant, precise, expensive. The future of warfare, if you can afford it.

**Signature Traits:**
- Armor-penetrating beam/pulse weapons
- Sleek white-and-chrome aesthetic
- Proprietary power cells (vendor lock-in)
- Near-silent firing signature
- Struggles against energy-dispersing shields

**Products:**
- Laser rifles and carbines
- Plasma sidearms
- Precision beam weapons
- Industrial cutting tools

---

#### Solaris Defense Systems

**Size:** Major | **Alignment:** Closely Aligned (Mesulea) | **HQ:** Mesulea (He-3 Orbital Complex)

Mesulea's premier weapons manufacturer, specializing in ship-mounted and heavy energy weapons. Their point-defense systems protect Mesulean convoys across the system. Less known for personal weapons, but their heavy equipment is unmatched.

**Character:** Professional, reliable, institutional. The backbone of Mesulean defense.

**Signature Traits:**
- Ship-scale energy weapons
- Integrated targeting systems

**Products:**

**Size:** Small | **Alignment:** Multinational | **HQ:** Atruna

Startup challenging Angelico's energy weapon dominance with more affordable designs. Their weapons lack Angelico's polish but cost half as much. Popular with mercenaries and security firms who want energy capability without the premium.
