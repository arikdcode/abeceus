## Corporate Relationships with Oziri

Corporations have complex relationships with the pirate factions:
- Some are targeted by raids
- Those doing business on [[locations/oziri/index|Oziri]] are protected from raiding by the [[factions/oziri-council|Council]]
- Getting immunity status from the Council is politically tricky
- Different factions have different interests in any given corporation
