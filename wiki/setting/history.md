# History & Timeline

## The Scream

The event that severed the Abeceus system from Earth. Details TBD.

## Major Eras

### Early Colonization
- Atruna settled by Earth colonists
- Scientific knowledge retained in databanks, but engineering expertise had to be rebuilt from scratch

### Expansion
- Mesulea colonized ~150 years after Atrunian planetfall
- Nuilea colonization began ~50 years after Mesulea

### Rise of Piracy
- As interplanetary trade grew, space piracy became a serious problem
- Abecean flares give pirates attack windows
- Wealthy pirates acquired advanced drives, retreated to outer system
- Oziri occupied by Pirate Lords

### The Oziri Conflict
- Mesulean and Atrunean ground assault on Oziri
- Failed due to labyrinthine defenses
- Led to the Pirate Compact

### Modern Era
- Oziri as functioning criminal nation-state
- Mesulea as dominant power via helium-3 control
- Nuilea as rising challenger
- Discovery of the Oziri Corridor
